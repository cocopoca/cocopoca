Based on the First Chart

Interpretation:

The pie chart illustrates the main purposes for which students at a certain university use their mobile phones for reading. According to the data, 59.5% of students primarily use their phones to learn new knowledge, making it the most common reason. Another 21.3% use their phones to pass time, 17.0% to obtain information, and 2.2% for other reasons.

Commentary:

  1. Educational Convenience: The majority (59.5%) of students use their phones for educational purposes, likely due to the convenience and accessibility of digital learning materials. Smartphones allow students to access a wide range of educational resources anytime and anywhere, enhancing their learning experience.
  2. Leisure Activities: A significant portion (21.3%) of students use their phones to pass time, indicating that smartphones serve as a primary source of entertainment. This may be due to the availability of various apps and media that provide engaging content, making it easier for students to relax and unwind.
  3. Information Access: The 17.0% of students who use their phones to obtain information highlight the role of smartphones as tools for quick and easy access to news and updates. This reflects the growing importance of staying informed in today’s fast-paced world.

Moreover, the increasing popularity of online platforms may have encouraged more people to purchase goods remotely, further driving up the demand for express delivery services.

In summary, the chart demonstrates a clear upward trajectory in both total and rural express delivery volumes in China from 2018 to 2020, reflecting broader shifts towards digital commerce and enhanced connectivity within the country.

Overall, the chart tells us that Beijing and Shanghai have done a good job in improving air quality, thanks to their strong environmental policies. However, Guangzhou’s air quality didn’t change much, and Shenzhen’s got worse. These results show that different cities need different plans to deal with pollution and keep the environment clean.

泛化

the pie chart illustrates the different purpose that students at a certain university when use their phones to reading. （错误）

to reading 该为for reading,purpose 加s,去掉when,purpose的从句用for which 引导

(正确表述)The pie chart illustrates the different purposes for which students at a certain university used their phones for reading in 2017.

the bar chart illustrates the different ways for which residents in a certain city choose to do exercise (错误)

ways的从句该用that引导，do多余

“The bar chart illustrates the different ways that residents in a certain city choose to exercise.”（正确）

The pie chart illustrates the different factors that customers in a certain city considered when choosing a restaurant in 2017 .（正确）

这里的when是对considered的补充

"The pie chart illustrates the different factors considered by customers in a certain city when choosing a restaurant in 2017."

这种表达方式将 "considered" 移动到从句的开头，使句子结构更加紧凑
